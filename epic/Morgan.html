Persona for Morgan Captain

Name: Morgan Captain

Age: 32

Gender: Male

Technical Ability/ Technology: Morgan is a power user who is an advanced developer as well. While at home and in the office Morgan prefers to browse with Google Chrome. He is using a 24" HP LED monitor, Dell Inspiron Gaming Desktop with the i7 intel core, OS: Microsoft Windows 10.If he travels he uses a Dell 15.6" laptop also using the i7 intel core, OS: Microsoft Windows 10. His phone is an Apple iPhone XR, OS: iOS 12.2.

Occupation: Morgan has worked double time since he entered the field. He has always been doing free lance work alongside his standard jobs. By doing so he has gained crucial experienced and highly valued at what he does has employers practically begging at his door. However, he is tired of going with what is given and wants to see all the options.

Attributes: Morgan is the life of the party on top of being a total work horse. People are constantly demanding his attention and its become tiresome. Morgan is ready for a break and wants to look at all his options before he enters the career field again. He learns extremely fast and is particularly interested in working in a more relaxed workplace rather than a typical cube job.

Problem: Morgan needs a great paying developer job, but on his terms. He wants to be able to get a full description of the job without applying first. Morgan needs to be able to save jobs he is interested in so he can choose his final company wisely, he also wants to see applications from a variety of websites.

User Story: As a user on this site I want to be able to find a job description, save jobs, as well as be taken to the application site.
