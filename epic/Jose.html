Purpose: To create a professional-user-friendly website for web developers. These developers will be able to create a profile to broadcast their skills and area of expertise. It will also allow employers, recruiters, and freelancers to set their user role for the website.

Audience:This site will be targeting professional individuals within web development and IT community, business owners, hiring managers, recruiters and web developers.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is for junior to advanced skill-based web developers to have a fast an easy way to find employment. It will also assist employers in posting and future recruiting.

Persona:

Situation: I am currently a technical recruiter who works 50 to 60 hour weeks. His stress level is high because it can be hard at time to find developers who fit the job requirements or has the needed skills for the job.

User Story As a recruiter, I am would like to have a website where I can see a brief snap-shot of a developers skills
